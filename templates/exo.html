Method of Finding
  • Imaging: Taking a direct image of the planet; very unlikely but possible
  • Radial Velocity: Observe host star for change in position caused by the gravitional pull of other planets
  • Transit: Measure for a dip in the host stars brightness, meaning something is passing by to block the light
